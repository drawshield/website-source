This section contains divisions containing multiple bars. These are different to ordinaries of multiple bars as the divsion bars are spread evenly across the whole field.

Note also that the number of "bars" can be specified for all of these variations, from anywhere between 1 and 64. Numbers below 4 look strange and numbers above 16 are pretty indistinguisable. Examples of some of the more useful numbers for barry are shown.
