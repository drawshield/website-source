The lions can be drawn in postures such as rampant, passant, cadent, couchant, courant, salient, sejeant, passant, statant, with its head guardant, reguardant and its tail fourche, nowed, saltire and possibly some others. You can specify any combination of the above and DrawShield will use the closest available, warning about anything it cannot handle.

There are other modifiers that may also be available, for example lion's head crowned.

Finally, note the discussion on the use of the term leopard, (which sometimes means "lion") under animals.
