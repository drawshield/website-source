These symbols are all from the excellent collection at http://bakadesign.dk/warhammer-40-000-icons/ and thanks are due to the original creators. Ownership and copyright of the designs resides with Games Workshop.

Each of the charges can be described in two ways, e.g.:

  • the symbol of the steel legion
  • the steel legion symbol

Note also that if you select the Pauldron shape the complete range of Warhammer paint colours can be used with these charges - if you want to use Warhammer colours in other shield shapes then you will need to enable them under the "Colour Scheme" section of the Preferences.
