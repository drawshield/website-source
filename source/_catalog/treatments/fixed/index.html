--- upLink: ../index.html nextLink: ../furs/index.html pageTitle: Treatments of Fixed Colours ---

These treatments have "objects" (roundels) that are a fixed colour. They are hence an exception to the general rule for specifying treatments and are specified in the blazon as [tincture] [treatment]. If you want to have roundels of other colours use the treatment roundelly.
