These treatments are made of objects that are repeated at even intervals. The first colour that is mentioned will be used to fill the background, the second mentioned colour is used for the objects themselves.

You can use any charge as the object used in the treatment by using the construction [tincture] semy of [charge] [tincture], see the semy of bees example for how the charges will be laid out.
