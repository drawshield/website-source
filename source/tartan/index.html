--- pageTitle: Design Your Own Tartan ---

Choose a colour and the number of threads for that colour (you need at least two!). Add more colours by clicking on the box with the plus sign. See your tartan build up as you add colours and widths. You can change existing colours and widths by clicking on them, or remove them.

Pay careful attention to the blazon as it is built up in the textarea - this will help you learn blazonry!
