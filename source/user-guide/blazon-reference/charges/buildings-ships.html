You might want to think of this category as "big stuff".

Buildings and Architectural Features

Arch, double arch, bascule, castle, church, cogwheel, cramp, millwheel, pillar, tower, tower triple towered, waterwheel, natural fountain, bridge, windmill, portcullis, quadrate cornice, well frame

The windmill can have sails of a different tincture.

The bridge can also be of two arches or of three arches

Many heraldic castles are described in great detail, but at the moment DrawShield treats them all equally and just draws the same castle.

The natural fountain is a spray of water from a stone bowl, as opposed to a heraldic fountain which is a type of roundel. There is also a fountain stone available which has no water flowing.

Ships and the Sea

Anchor, caravel, coracle, fishing boat, ship, galley

Many heraldic ships are described in great detail, but at the moment DrawShield treats them all equally and just draws the same ship.
