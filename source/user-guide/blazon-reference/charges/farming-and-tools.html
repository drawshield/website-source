This is a fairly arbitrary collection of charges that I think are tools and are often to do with farming.

Farm Related Items

Corn sheaf, spoked-wheel, eel-basket, harrow, fish hook, horseshoe, beehive, plough, ploughshare, windmill, wicker fence, shepherd's crook, crook of Basel

The corn sheaf is also known as a garb

Tools and related Items

Anvil, brey, pair of calipers, compass, embroiderer's broach, flail, fleam, hammer, forge hammer, hemp-break, key, ladder, millrind, padlock, pair of keys, set square scythe, sledge hammer, artists brush, quill pen

Features and Modifiers

Are not typical for this kind of charge, but the brey may be open, closed.
