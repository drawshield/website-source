The arms of Thomas Alfred Curley, another recent grant from the CHA. I like these.

Arms: Vert on a chevron cotised Or three blue columbine flowers Azure;
Crest: Issuant from a coronet of crosses patté and oak leaves Or, a demi-dragon Vert incensed Gules gorged with a coronet flory Or;
Motto: HONOR ET HONESTAS;
