On this day, in 1628, was born John Granville, 1st Earl of Bath

Arms: Gules, three clarions or (Grenville) impaling Azure, a pile ermine (Wyche, family of his wife).
Crest: On a cap of maintenance on a helm on an earl's coronet, a griffin passant or
Supporters: two winged griffins rampant or
Motto: Futurum invisibile ("The future is unseen").
