In solidarity with the people of Louisiana, who've been suffering with major floods lately.

These historical arms are no longer used, but the modern seal features the same pelican and a variant of the motto.

Argent, a brown pelican in her piety, in chief scales, all proper.
Motto: justice, union and confidence
