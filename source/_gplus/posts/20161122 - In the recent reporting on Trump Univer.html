In the recent reporting on Trump University, I got to see their color logo/arms. Those are almost heraldic for a change!

Quaterly Gules and Or, a lion rampant Sable; in chief two chevronels of the same; within a bordure Or

There is one tiny thing in this achievement though - the lion is all Sable, whereas they are typically going to be langued and armed Gules.
Is there a way to blazon that?
