In support for the city of Brussels, who experienced terrorist attacks last week, here are the arms of the city.

The blazon is in French, which I'm translating on the fly now

Arms: Gules, St-Michael Or slaying the Devil Sable.
Crest: A count's coronet
Supporters : Two lions Or, each holding one of the banners saltirewise.
The dexter flag show the arms of Brabant, the sinister one being the arms of the city.
Compartment: A mound Vert
