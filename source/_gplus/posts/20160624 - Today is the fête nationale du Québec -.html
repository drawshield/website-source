Today is the fête nationale du Québec - so the follow flag is going to be flown a looooot.

The official blazon is "d'azur à la croix d'argent cantonnée de quatre fleurs de lys du même"

A more 'modern' blazon in English would be 'Azure, a cross between four fleur-de-lys Argent'.
