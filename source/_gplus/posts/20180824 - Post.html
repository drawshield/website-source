Banner of St Edward the Confessor - Westminster Abbey Designed by Christopher Webb, this banner was presented in 1945 by Eulalie Buckmaster in memory of her father. An inscription on a plate on the banner pole reads, ‘In memory of Walter Selby Buckmaster October 16 1872: October 30 1942’. Made from gold cloth and embroidered in rich silks, it depicts a figure of St Edward the Confessor holding a model of the Abbey, which he built, and a charter
