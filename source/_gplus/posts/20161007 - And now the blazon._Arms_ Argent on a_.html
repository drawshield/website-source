And now the blazon.

Arms: Argent on a pallet Gules between two pallets Azure each charged with an organ pipe, a conductor’s baton Argent;

Crest: A Salt Bank schooner proper its hull Azure flying a streamer paly Gules and Argent;

Supporters:Two great blue herons each holding in its beak a lady’s slipper flower proper and issuant from barry wavy Argent and Azure;

Motto: IN CONSONANTIA;
