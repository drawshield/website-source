Name-geeks might be interested in this:

Adolf Socin 1903. "Mittelhochdeutsches Namenbuch: nach oberrheinischen Quellen des zwölften und dreizehnten Jahrhunderts " (Helbing & Lichtenhahn)
http://reader.digitale-sammlungen.de/de/fs3/object/display/bsb10930484_00001.html

You can download the entire book in PDF format. There is a tiny British flag in the top corner of the first page that will translate the page into English for you, so you can certify that you're using it for research, generate a download code, enter it, and wait for your PDF to generate so you can save it out.  Chrome's helpful "do you want to translate this page?" also helped me a lot, as the site is in German.
