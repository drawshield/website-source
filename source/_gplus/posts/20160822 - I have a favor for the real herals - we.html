I have a favor for the real herals - we're working on our arms and this is the best design we're thinking of.

Let me know what you think, and if you have enough spare time and talent to draw it, that'd be even more awesome.

Arms: per fesse dancetty Azure and Argent; in chief a foi, in base an open book inscribed with 'veritas'; on the honor point, a Greek cross, all counterchanged.
Crest: Issuant from a coronet fleury Or, a demi snowy owl displayed Proper

Alternative design for the arms:
Arms: per fesse dancetty Azure and Argent; in chief an open book inscribed with 'veritas' between two Greek crosses; in base a foi, all counterchanged.
