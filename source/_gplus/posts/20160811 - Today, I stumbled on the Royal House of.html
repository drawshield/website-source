Today, I stumbled on the Royal House of Georgia. Georgia is a former monarchy and may become one in the future again.

Arms: Quarterly Gules and Azure, a cross Argent.
1st: a sling Argent stone Or, an harp of the last
2nd: A scepter and sword in saltire, in chief an orb, all Proper
3rd: scales Or
4: St George on a horse slaying a dragon Argent, halo Or
Over all, an estucheon Gules charged with the Seamless Tunic Argent
Crest: The crown of Georgia proper
Supporters: two lions Or
Motto: Tunica illa inconsultilis desupper contexta per totum
