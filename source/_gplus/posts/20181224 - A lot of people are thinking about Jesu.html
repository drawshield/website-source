A lot of people are thinking about Jesus nowadays, for some reason ;)

So lets get our creativity... imagine what his coat of arms should be... no need to stick to tradition.

My humble contribution: "Argent, a label Azure".
Why? "God is light; in him there is no darkness at all." (1John 1:5), hence the crisp white field. The label is for obvious reasons. I picked Azure because of rule of tinctures and because I like blue.

(Drawshield has a bug and renders the label Gules it seems)
