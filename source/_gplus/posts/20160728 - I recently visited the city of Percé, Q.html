I recently visited the city of Percé, Québec, Canada. It is one of the few places in that area who have arms granted by the CHA (I saw a low of arms and quasy-arms that were assumed and more in the French style)

Arms Azure a cod embowed fesswise between in chief a representation of the Percé Rock and in base a trilobite (Synphoroides biardi) palewise all Or;

Crest A traditional Gaspé fishing boat Argent rigged Azure issuing from a mural coronet with three battlements Or, masoned Azure;

Supporters Two gannets proper;
