As the Commonwealth meeting is over in Malta, let's have a look at the arms of that country.

Crest A mural crown with a Sally port and five Vedettes.
Escutcheon Per pale Argent and Gules, a representation of George Cross Argent fimbriated Gules in Dexter Chief
Supporters Dexter, An olive Branch; Sinister, A Palm branch in vert all in their proper colours
