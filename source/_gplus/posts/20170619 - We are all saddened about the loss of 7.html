We are all saddened about the loss of 79+ lives in Grenfell Tower in London, which is located in the Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea. Here are the arms of that borough.

The blazon is not specified, so here is what I'm guessing.

Arms: Gules, a mitre Or; on a chief Ermine three Ducal coronets Or
Crest: A broom plant Proper
Supporters: on the dexter, a boar Sable langued Gules and tusked Or; on the sinister winged bull Argent hooved and armed Or
Compartment: a hill Vert
Motto: Quam Bonum in Unum Habitare, which means 'What a good thing it is to dwell in unity'.
