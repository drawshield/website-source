In solidarity with the people of Eastern Canada, who has been dealing with major floods lately, here is the arms of one of the flooded cities, Gatineau

I couldn't find the blazon, nor a high-res image, so here's my best guess.

Arms: Or, a fesse wavy Azure between three fir trees Vert within a bordure Vert billeté Or;
Coronet: a municipal coronet Or
Supporters: two maple tree branches Vert, meeting in saltire at the point, tied with a ribbon Gules.
