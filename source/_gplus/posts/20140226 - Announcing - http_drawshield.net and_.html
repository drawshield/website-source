Announcing - drawshield.net and Drawshield3 pre-alpha

Hi Folks! Drawshield has a new home! Or at least a new name for its existing home...

I must be doing something right because someone has "cybersquatted" drawshield.com ! I always felt .net was more appropriate anyway, so that's what it is. At the moment it just redirects to karlwilcox.com/drawshield (which will still be available). I would like it to be a stand-alone domain but I need to do some more research about how to do that.

If you visit drawshield.net you will see a new tab down the side of the book, titled Analyse . This is a back-door into a very early version of drawshield3 - don't get too excited it doesn't draw anything yet, just does some analysis of the blazon.
