Another beautiful recent grant from the CHA. I love the cross made of whale tails. The crest, less so, but the arms rock.

Arms : Azure a cross of four whale tails conjoined Argent, a chief per fess sapinagé Vert and Argent;

Crest : On a sandy mount set with marram grass, a puffin statant wings displayed proper;

Motto : CHERISH HONOUR;

http://reg.gg.ca/heraldry/pub-reg/project-pic.asp?lang=e&ProjectID=2931&ProjectElementID=10305

Clarke, Paul Philip [Individual]
