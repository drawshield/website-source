The commonwealth meeting is on. Now looking at the arms of the smallest commonweatlh realm, Tuvalu

Per fess Azure and Or in chief upon Grass issuant a representation of an Ellice Maneapa or meeting house all proper and in base four Barrulets wavy Azure, a Brodure Or charged with Banana Leaves and Mitre Sea Shells placed alternately proper together

Motto: TUVALU MO TE ATUA (Tuvalu for the Almighty).
