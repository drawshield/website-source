Happy Independence Day to my American friends!

How would you blazon these arms? I took the liberty of reworking at the American Heraldry Society put.

ARMS. Pally of thirteen argent and gules; a chief, azure;
Supporter: behind the estucheon, an American eagle displayed proper, holding in his dexter talon an olive branch, and in his sinister a bundle of thirteen arrows, all proper, and in his beak a scroll Or, inscribed with this motto
CREST. A hurt charged with thirteen mullets Argent 1-4-3-4-1 surrounded by a glory Or breaking through a cloud Proper
Motto: “E pluribus Unum.”
