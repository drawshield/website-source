Season's Greetings!

As my gift to my faithful users I would like to present you with this list of over 150 new charges! I would like to credit the Traceable Heraldic Art resource for all of these new additions. I have modified the DrawShield to work with the images from that site, once they have been re-named to match DrawShield naming conventions. I am intending to add almost the whole catalog over the coming months. My grateful thanks to all the original artists and the curators of the Heraldic Art website.

I am in the process of updating the "charge finder" and the indvidual pages within the reference section of the user guide but until that is finished the complete set of new charges can be found below.
