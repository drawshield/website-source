--- pageTitle: Teach Me Blazonry ---

Choose the parts from the questions below and watch how your blazon is built up. If you are new to heraldry then choose "A Simple Shield" - this has a small number of options and will carefully guide you through the process. If you want to access many more features (but answer many more questions!) then choose "A Complex Shield".

Pay careful attention to the blazon as it is built up in the textarea - this will help you learn blazonry!
