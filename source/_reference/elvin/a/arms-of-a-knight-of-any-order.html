Arms of A Knight Of Any Order

Arms of A Knight Of Any Order. Consist of his paternal coat in a shield surrounded with the insignia of the Order of which he is a Knight ; and, if married, the arms of his wife must be placed in a distinct shield impaled with his own, as P. 16, f. 21.
