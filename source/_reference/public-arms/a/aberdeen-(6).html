ABERDEEN, Trades Incorporations of. The different Trades incorporations of Aberdeen matriculated their arms in Lyon Register in 1682. Refer to Bakers, Butchers, Hammermen, Shoemakers, Tailors, Weavers, Wrights and Coopers.
