INVERKEITHING (Fifeshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. It has several seals, but the one which seems to do duty represents upon waves of the sea an ancient one-masted vessel, the sail furled, and within the legend,

" S' commune Burgi de Invirkethyn."
