GRAHAMSTOWN, See of (S. Africa). Argent, a cross gules, thereon a sword in pale, the blade wavy proper, in the dexter canton an anchor sable.

[Arms formerly used were argent, a saltire gules, over all an anchor sable. There is no authority for either version.]
