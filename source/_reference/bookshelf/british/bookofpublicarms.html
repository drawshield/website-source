This is the essential reference for all arms that belong to entities other than families. There is a brief introduction and then a simple alphabetical list of entries, the majority with clear illustrations. Available from archive.org in a text-searchable PDF and other formats at http://archive.org/details/bookofpublicarms00foxd.

Good scan quality, almost all pages clearly readable, image quality good to excellent. OCR accuracy 95%+
